*Spring 2024 (First year PhD)*

## Context
During spring 2024, I continued the course co-supervised with [Vincent Bonnet](https://www.linkedin.com/in/vincent-david-bonnet/?originalSubdomain=fr) during [fall 2023](ludovicdematteis.github,io/2023-fall-BUT). I gave practical sessions of the course *Advanced robotics* at the [University Institute of Technology of Toulouse (IUT)](https://iut.univ-tlse3.fr/genie-electrique-informatique-industrielle). Students where third year of higher education with major in electrical engineering and Industrial Information (GEII).

## Content of the course 
### Advanced to robotics
This courses aims at going further into the understanding of the motion of robots. Different controls were studied on a simple 1 DOF robot. The students implemented **gravity compensation**, **PID controlers** and **force control**, first in a simulation and then on a real robot.
This courses ends with a longer project where students worked on a 3-DOF robot and implement the controllers studied before.
They also worked on controlling a motor for the end effector, simulating a sanding task where force control is required. Some students also worked on a teleoperation problem by reading the position in one robot and controlling the other robot somewhere else in the room.
