I am a PhD student in the [Gepetto team](https://www.laas.fr/public/fr/gepetto) at [LAAS-CNRS](https://www.laas.fr) under the supervision of [Nicolas Mansard](https://gepettoweb.laas.fr/nmansardx``). My work will mainly resolve around "Model Predictive Control for the locomotion of bipedal robots with complex actuation", which includes problematics on optimisation, automation and motion planning.
I currently focus more on contact implicit motions using Path Integral approaches.

I graduated from [ENS Paris Saclay](https://ens-paris-saclay.fr/) in 2023 with a Master degree in applied mathematics ([MVA](https://www.master-mva.com/)) and computer science and another in electrical engineering. I also passed (first position nation-wide in 2022) competitive exams to teach engineering science in french higher education. Find more about my education in the [CV](https://ludovicdematteis.github.io/cv/) section of this site.
